## Slide 2: Problem Statement
* **Current Scenario:** KSP manages vast crime data from 1100+ police stations but relies on static dashboards and manual database queries.
* **Core Pain Points:**
  * Inability to run natural language queries in Kannada/English.
  * Hidden linkages between suspects, UPI transactions, and cross-border locations are hard to trace.
  * Absence of proactive/early warning systems and explainable reasoning paths for investigators.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
* **Solution Concept:** An integrated, secure intelligence workstation combining NLP, relational mapping, and interactive visualization.
* **Value Proposition:** 
  * Bridges technical gaps by allowing investigators to directly talk to the database.
  * Uncovers complex cyber-financial networks (Hawala & Crypto endpoints).
  * Accelerates decision-making with automated Modus Operandi matches.

---

## Slide 4: Key Features
* **Bilingual Chat & Voice Q&A:** Native English & Kannada voice recognition with dynamic audio waveforms.
* **Criminal Network mapping:** Force-directed interaction graphs identifying accomplices, vehicles, and phone records.
* **UPI Money Trails (Sankey Flow):** Direct visualization from victim loss to final money cash-out points.
* **MO Lead Matcher & Radar Profile:** Match crime descriptions to criminal dossiers and verify behavioral similarities on a 6-axis radar map.
* **Case Storyboard Board:** Interactive mind-map to drag, drop, and link case elements.

---

## Slide 5: Explainable AI & Governance
* **Reasoning Transparency:** Shows the AI's internal thoughts and the exact SQL queries executed behind every response.
* **Role-Based Security:** Gated logins for Investigators, Analysts, Superintendents, and Administrators with custom access privileges.
* **Comprehensive Audit Trail:** Captures every query, timestamp, target suspect, and action for compliance and legal accountability.

---

## Slide 6: Technology Stack & Architecture
* **Backend:** Python, FastAPI, Uvicorn
* **Frontend:** HTML5, Vanilla CSS, Vanilla JavaScript (Lightweight, No heavyweight node modules)
* **Visualizations:** Apache ECharts, Leaflet.js (OpenStreetMap)
* **APIs:** Web Speech API, PDF Print Engine
* **Deployment:** Zoho Catalyst Platform

## Slide 7: Impact & Proactive Intelligence
* **Operational Efficiency:** Reduces lead generation time from days to seconds.
* **Cross-Border Coordination:** Flagging systems alert neighboring state border patrols.
* **Preventative Warnings:** Demographic correlations flag socioeconomic factors (literacy vs. crime types) to guide social policy interventions.
